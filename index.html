About Me

I attended the University of North Texas and graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree (German major, Spanish minor), and about all it landed me was a cashiering job at Target. Not very exciting. So, I started to code and develop web pages for about a year now. I know HTML/CSS, Processing JS, some Python, and a little bit of C#. It would be nice to actually be able to earn some money doing it one day, but for now, it is just a hobby.

I currently live in norteast Texas with my husband and three children. I also work at the Target in Texarkana, and in my spare time, I like to cook and program.

How I got into Programming

Oh, yeah, I forgot to mention that I'm actually studying to be a teacher. I wanted to be a math teacher, since I was rather good at it in school. But I learned really quickly that I had forgotten a lot of what I learned in school. Well, I was telling a teacher of my plight, and they recommended Khan Academy. So, one day, I decided to try their Hour of Code. From then on, I was hooked on coding!
